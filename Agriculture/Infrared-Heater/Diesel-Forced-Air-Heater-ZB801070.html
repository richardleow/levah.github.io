4. With flame sensor.
5. If no oil , heater will cut off the power.
6. Fixed Oil display.
7. Cut-off protection,re-plugged in will not work automatically.
8. Low pressure automization,combustion completely.
9. Easy Maintenance.
10. Suitable for heating warehouse, factories, and workshops.
